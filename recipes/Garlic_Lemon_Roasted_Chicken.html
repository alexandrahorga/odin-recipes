Garlic Lemon Roasted Chicken

Description

This garlic lemon roasted chicken is a simple and flavorful dish that combines bright citrus notes with savory garlic and herbs. It roasts to a golden, crispy finish while staying tender and juicy inside.

The recipe is easy to prepare and uses everyday ingredients. It works great as a family dinner and pairs nicely with roasted vegetables, rice, or a fresh salad.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (3–4 lb)
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon (zested and halved)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 teaspoon rosemary

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Pat the chicken dry and place it in a roasting pan.
  3. Mix the olive oil, garlic, lemon zest, salt, pepper, thyme, and rosemary.
  4. Rub the mixture all over the chicken and place the lemon halves inside the cavity.
  5. Roast for 50–70 minutes or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (75°C).
  6. Let the chicken rest for 10 minutes before carving.
